Doctor Bios

Dr. Linda Puggs


A dedicated veterinarian at Dr. Pug Animal Clinic, Dr. Linda has a lifelong love for pugs. Her expertise and passion for these adorable canines make her an exceptional caregiver. With specialized training in small animal medicine, Dr. Puggs provides personalized care, tailored treatments, and a comforting environment for every pug and pet she treats. She actively supports pug rescue organizations, promotes responsible pet ownership, and advocates for pug health. Dr. Linda's commitment to the well-being of pugs makes her a trusted professional at the clinic and a beloved advocate for pug lovers everywhere.

Dr. Jeffrey Doggert


A recent addition to the team at Dr. Pug Animal Clinic, Dr. Jefferey brings specialized expertise in canine respiratory care and anesthesia. He works closely with Dr. Linda to deliver exceptional veterinary care, focusing on advanced respiratory treatments and safe anesthesia administration. With his extensive training in this specific field, Dr. Jefferey enhances the clinic's ability to provide comprehensive and specialized care to pugs and other beloved pets. His dedication to staying updated with the latest advancements ensures that patients receive optimal care, promoting their well-being during procedures and surgeries.
